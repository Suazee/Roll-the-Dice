🎲 "ROLL THE DICE" 🎲 RULES


🎲 The game has 2 players, playing in rounds

🎲 In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as he whishes. Each result get added to the ROUND score

🎲 BUT, if the player rolls a 1, all the ROUND score gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn

🎲 The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that the ROUND score gets added to the GLOBAL score. After that, it's the next player's turn

🎲 The first player to reach 100 points on GLOBAL score wins the game


Goodluck 🍀
